This was one of my favorite reads of 2023 for sure. It provided great personal development advice grounded in science and psychology.

### Improving your personal life

Most of the advice that the book gives is going to lead to life-changing improvements that you can try to become more in control of your emotions and your brain in general. All of this was achieved by the author constantly referencing societal and psychological norms that are very easy to understand and apply. It was also good to see how these concepts have influenced humans and animals from the present and the past.

### You are in control

This was my favorite message from the book. There are a lot of topics and general advice given that may seem daunting at first, but the main idea is that, as humans, we are in control of our own time and emotions. I personally tried some of the ideas in this book and failed many times, but nothing stops me from trying again. You are in control.

### A hidden gem?

Not only can this book be considered a hidden gem, but I recently discovered that the author also has a great podcast on YouTube. Just look up the Cal Newport Podcast and you will easily find it. All killer, no filler, just like the Sum 41 record.

### Should you read this?

Yes, but most importantly, try to apply the concepts outlined in the book and if you fail, make sure to try again, it may take a couple of tries for those to stick.
